About

Opeyemi Taiwo Adeniran is a certified data scientist, AI engineer, and researcher who is passionate about solving organizational and community problems through artificial intelligence.

Moreover, Opeyemi has 13 course certificates in computer vision, deep learning, and natural language processing from educational institutions, in addition to professional certifications in data science from IBM and AI from Microsoft. Opeyemi has worked as a data scientist and an AI engineer with companies, including Omdena Inc. as an AI evangelist and partnership manager, and is presently a graduate student researcher at the National Science Foundation (NSF) where she develops experiments on computer architecture courses.

  • City: MaryLand, USA
  • Degree: Masters in computer science

Furthermore, Opeyemi is a community lead at the Global AI Hub, a Google ambassador for women techmakers, and an IBM Masters Fellow, an award given to exceptional Masters students who want to make their mark in promising and disruptive technologies. Opeyemi enjoys assisting people who want to pursue a career in information technology and founded SHE MODEL TECH, a nonprofit organization dedicated to educating and mentoring women who want to start or advance their career in information technology.
